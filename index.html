Acne

Acne is a common skin condition caused by clogged pores, excess oil, bacteria, and inflammation. It often appears on the face, chest, and back.

Rashes

Rashes are changes in skin color or texture caused by irritation, infection, allergies, or underlying conditions.

Eczema (Dermatitis)

Eczema is a chronic inflammatory condition that causes dry, itchy, and irritated skin. It often flares due to triggers like stress or allergens.

Psoriasis

Psoriasis is an autoimmune condition that leads to rapid skin cell buildup, resulting in thick, scaly patches.

Rosacea

Rosacea causes facial redness, flushing, and sometimes bumps, often triggered by heat, stress, or certain foods.

Fungal Infections

Fungal infections occur when fungi grow on the skin, commonly affecting warm, moist areas such as feet or groin.

Hives

Hives are raised, itchy welts caused by allergic reactions, stress, or infections. They often appear suddenly and fade within hours.
