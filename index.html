Queen’s University Dermatology Club

Derma-T is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting skin health, hygiene, and dermatology awareness on campus. We provide evidence-based education, practical guidance, and accessible resources to support informed skin care decisions for students.

About Us

Derma-T at Queen’s University is a student-run dermatology club focused on educating and empowering students through reliable, science-backed skin health information. Our goal is to reduce misinformation surrounding skin care while fostering confidence, wellness, and healthy habits. Through educational events, curated resources, and community outreach, we aim to make dermatology knowledge approachable and relevant to student life.

Programs & Activities

Educational Workshops & Talks

Interactive workshops and speaker sessions featuring professionals, medical students, and guest experts covering topics such as acne management, sun protection, and common skin conditions.

Weekly Skin Health Resources

Student-friendly articles, guides, and informational posts designed to break down dermatology concepts, debunk common myths, and share evidence-based skin care tips.

Community Outreach Initiatives

Campus-wide initiatives and collaborations that promote hygiene awareness, preventative skin care, and inclusive wellness through campaigns, events, and partnerships.

Skin Health Resources

Understanding Acne

Learn about the causes of acne, evidence-based treatment options, and how to separate common myths from medical facts.

Sunscreen & Sun Protection

A practical guide to choosing the right sunscreen, understanding SPF, and applying sun protection effectively.

Hydration & Moisturizers

Discover how moisturizers work, how to choose products for different skin types, and tips for maintaining healthy skin balance.
