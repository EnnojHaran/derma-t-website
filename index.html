Dermatology Education for Students

Derma-T is a Queen’s University student-run club focused on skin health education, hygiene awareness, and accessible dermatology resources.

About Derma-T

Derma-T aims to bridge the gap between students and reliable dermatology knowledge. We host events, share educational content, and collaborate with professionals to promote skin health awareness.
