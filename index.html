About Derma-T

Derma-T is a student initiative designed to promote skin health and hygiene through education, workshops, and community outreach.

Our Purpose

We aim to spread awareness about skin health and hygiene, reduce stigma around common skin conditions, and encourage safe and informed skincare habits.

Our Goals

  • Share weekly educational posts
  • Host guest speakers and Q&A sessions
  • Run workshops and outreach events
  • Promote accessible hygiene and skincare habits
