About Derma-T

Derma-T is a student-led nonprofit project dedicated to promoting education around skin health, hygiene, and awareness. We aim to provide students with insight into dermatology, opportunities to connect with healthcare professionals, and ways to positively impact the community through educational, service, and financial initiatives.

We are committed to raising awareness of skin health issues both on campus and in the broader community beyond Kingston.

Our Goals

  • Weekly educational posts on common skin conditions and preventive care
  • Exposure to dermatology and healthcare through guest speakers
  • Community service through hygiene kit creation and outreach
  • Development of leadership, teamwork, and organizational skills

Planned Activities

  • Guest speaker series with dermatologists and healthcare professionals
  • Educational workshops on skincare, hygiene, and sun protection
  • Community outreach and hygiene kit distribution
  • Fundraising and awareness campaigns
  • Collaborations with student and community organizations
