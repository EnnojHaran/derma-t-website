About Derma-T

Derma-T is a student-led nonprofit initiative dedicated to promoting education around skin health, hygiene, and dermatology awareness. Our goal is to provide students with a strong foundation in dermatology while creating meaningful community impact through education, outreach, and service.

We aim to raise awareness of skin health issues both on campus and beyond Kingston through evidence-based education and community engagement.

Our Goals

  • Promote evidence-based education on skin health and hygiene
  • Provide exposure to dermatology and healthcare careers
  • Support community outreach through hygiene initiatives
  • Develop leadership, teamwork, and organizational skills

Planned Activities

  • Guest speaker sessions with dermatologists and healthcare professionals
  • Educational workshops on skincare, hygiene, and sun protection
  • Community outreach and hygiene kit distribution
  • Fundraising and skin health awareness campaigns
  • Collaborations with student and community organizations
